Privacy Policy for Ask AI Assistant App created by KappsTech

Effective date: April 16, 2023

1. What information do we collect?

We do not collect any user information. We only receive the questions that are sent via the internet to our AI assistant.

2. How do we use the information we receive?

We use the information we receive (i.e., the questions asked by the user) solely for the purpose of providing answers to the user. We do not use the information for any other purpose.

3. Do we share the information we receive?

We do not share the information we receive with any third party. We may, however, share the information with our employees or contractors who are involved in the development or maintenance of our app. We require our employees and contractors to maintain the confidentiality of the information we receive.

4. How do we protect the information we receive?

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we receive from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use industry-standard security measures to protect the information, including encryption and firewalls.

5. Children's privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our app.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@kappstech.com.
